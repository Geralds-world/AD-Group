Mealie King

Mealie King is one of South Africa's leading maize meal distributors, known for its commitment to quality and excellence in the production of maize meal products. ITAU is a renowned supplier of super maize meal, catering to a variety of food industries with its exceptional quality products. With a focus on providing top-notch products, ITAU ensures that its maize meal and flour products meet the highest standards of excellence, helping businesses succeed in their culinary endeavors.
